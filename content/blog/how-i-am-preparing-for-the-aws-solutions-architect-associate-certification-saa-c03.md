### Climbing the Cloud: My Path to Conquering the AWS Solutions Architect Associate Certification

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*R8D8N-BL3SdUbHthNPYm_A.png)

Hello everyone!

Most of the articles around AWS Certifications are either “How I Passed X Certification” or “How To Pass X Certification”. But, today what I will be doing is walking you through my preparations for the [AWS Solutions Architect Associate Certification (SAA-C03)](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-solutions-architect-associate/) and how I am actually studying and preparing for it.

## 1\. I Bought A Course

Although you can benefit from the AWS Training and Certification resources, it is always preferable to take an external course, especially from Stephan Maarek. That’s what I did when I prepared and passed the AWS Cloud Practitioner Certification.

So, I went ahead and bought the course:   
[Ultimate AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate SAA-C03](https://www.udemy.com/course/aws-certified-solutions-architect-associate-saa-c03/)

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*CDAjkUuyCre2JONmc5_F_w.jpeg)

This guy is very credible and knowledgeable of the material he is teaching. He even passed this certification with a score of **984/1000** which shows how good he is when it comes to AWS and DevOps.
